Delete your Spotted account

Spotted — a wildlife life-list app by Melody Patin

Spotted lets you delete your account and all associated data at any time, directly in the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Spotted app.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

If you can no longer access the app, email melody.patin.dev@gmail.com from the email address on your account and we will delete it for you.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes, from our servers:

  • your account and email address;
  • your profile (first name, home country, visa type, arrival and trip details, avatar thumbnail);
  • your collection (spotted species, favourites, sightings, notes, and badges);
  • your friend connections and invite code.

Photos you added to sightings are stored only on your device and are removed when you delete them in the app or uninstall it — they are never uploaded to our servers.

Data retention

Account data is deleted immediately on request. Copies may remain in encrypted server backups for up to 30 days before being permanently erased.

See also our Privacy Policy.
